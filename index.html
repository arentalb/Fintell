Have questions?

How do I create an invoice using Fintell?

Creating an invoice in Fintell is easy. After signing in, click on the "Invoices" tab, then select "Create Invoice." Fill in the necessary details, including client information, products or services, and amounts. You can also customize the template to match your brand. Click "Save," and your invoice is ready to send.

Is Fintell's payment processing secure?

Yes, Fintell prioritizes payment security. We integrate with trusted payment processors to ensure that your transactions are protected. Your financial data is handled with the highest level of security, complying with industry standards.

Can I track expenses using Fintell?

Absolutely! Fintell offers a robust expense tracking feature. You can record and categorize expenses, which helps you manage your costs effectively and gain valuable insights into your financial health.

How can Fintell help me get paid faster?

Fintell streamlines the invoicing process, making it easy for your clients to pay online. We also offer automated payment reminders, reducing late payments and ensuring you get paid faster.

Is there a mobile app for Fintell?

Yes, Fintell offers a mobile app for both iOS and Android devices. You can access your account, create and send invoices, and manage your finances on the go.

Can I integrate Fintell with other accounting software?

Fintell is designed to work seamlessly with various accounting software. We offer integrations with popular accounting tools, making it easy to synchronize your financial data.

How often are software updates released for Fintell?

We regularly release updates to enhance Fintell's features and security. You can expect new features and improvements on a monthly basis.
